Release Notes: 2017-03-14
============================

Thanks for upgrading to the latest version of the ALKS CLI!

- You can now list all of your available ALKS accounts, both standard and IAM by running the new accounts command: `alks developer accounts`.
- You can now see where the password is being accessed from in verbose (`-v`) mode.
